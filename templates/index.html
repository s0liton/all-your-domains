Introduction

This app was created to provide Okta engineers a quick and easy way to acquire a valid TLD for their POCs. Domains are free to use and are valid for 1-12 months.

These domains are usable to configure domains for Office 365, Okta Custom Domain, Salesforce Custom Domains, etc...

What about DNS entries?

At this time, you must self-manage DNS using Cloudflare or another DNS provider. In future versions, I plan to integrate DNS functionality directly against the service. Cloudflare is free, provides a very nice user interface, and boasts many features such as DNS proxying and DDoS protection.

Do I/We/Okta own this domain?

No. The domain service used is called Freenom. They provide free domains against a specific set of TLDs, but they retain ownership of the domain. You are simply "using it". This is typically not an issue for a POC, which is short lived.

Cool. How do I use it?

All you have to do is register with your Okta e-mail address. Once approved, you will be able to create and update domains as necessary.
